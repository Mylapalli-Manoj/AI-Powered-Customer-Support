ABC Technologies - Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. A password reset link will be sent to your registered email and is valid for 24 hours.

Q: Can I change my subscription plan?
A: Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your subscription anytime from Account Settings. Downgrades become effective from the next billing cycle.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise customers.

Q: How long does a refund take?
A: Once approved by a human supervisor, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.

Q: What should I do if I cannot log in?
A: Verify your email and password, reset your password if necessary, and ensure your account is active. If the issue continues, contact Technical Support.

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes. Customer data is encrypted during transmission and storage, and we follow industry-standard security and privacy practices.

Q: Where can I get technical help?
A: Technical Support assists with application crashes, installation problems, login issues, file upload errors, and software configuration.

Q: How can I contact customer support?
A: Customers can contact ABC Technologies through the customer portal, email, or live chat. Support is available 24/7.
