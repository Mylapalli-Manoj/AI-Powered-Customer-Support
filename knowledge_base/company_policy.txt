ABC Technologies - Company Policy Document

General Support Policy:
ABC Technologies provides cloud-based business management software with 24/7 customer support. Customers can contact support through chat, email, or the customer portal.

Refund Policy:
Customers are eligible for a full refund within 14 days of purchase if they are not satisfied with the service. Refund requests after 14 days require manager approval and are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. All refund requests must be reviewed and approved by a human supervisor before processing.

Subscription Cancellation:
Customers may cancel their subscription at any time through their account settings. Cancellation requests for annual plans requested within 30 days of renewal require supervisor approval.

Account Closure:
Account closure requests are permanent and require supervisor approval. Customer data is retained for 90 days before permanent deletion in accordance with company policy.

Compensation Requests:
Compensation requests related to service outages, billing errors, or inconvenience must be reviewed by management before approval.

Escalation Policy:
Requests involving refunds, account closure, subscription cancellation, compensation, or escalation to management cannot be approved automatically and must be reviewed by a human supervisor.

Privacy Policy:
Customer information is stored securely and is only used to provide support services. Personal information is never shared with third parties without authorization.
